# ChatGPT Project Flow

Use this app like this:

1. Record your routines, study, work, emotion, and mistakes.
2. Click `GPT Pack`.
3. Upload the downloaded markdown file to your ChatGPT Project.
4. Ask ChatGPT to review your patterns, praise what went well, and suggest your smallest restart action.

Good prompt:

Review this Return OS project pack. Tell me:
- what I did well
- what pattern is hurting me
- what one small action I should do next
- what time of day I tend to work best
